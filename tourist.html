Ancol Monument

Located in North Jakarta, Taman Impian Jaya Ancol to be precise, this monument consists of 3 pilar-like structure where a writing for the future generations to see. The monument itself is not the only tourist attraction in the area, Ancol is the place for tourist as there are also waterparks and a recreational park for people to enjoy

National Museum

We have seen the national monument before, now we will go to the national museum. The national museum is located in the Central Jakarta and serves a lot of historical collections from all over the country. The building also has a Greek-style inner courtyard, letting in natural light and air.

Aesthetic Element Monument

A contemporary statue located in front of the most “corrupted” building in Indonesia. This statue is a symbol of the incarnation of the true Indonesian man, the wishes and hopes that are conveyed through the people's representative institutions. Although this wish is not being represented by the people inhabiting the building near this monument, the monument still has a lot of visitors. Though their intentions are still unclear, this monument is often visited by college student and some “upset” civilians. They usually came together as a group to, lets just say, “enjoy” the beauty of this monument which sometimes is not allowed to enter by the authorities and being left hanging at the gates for hours.
